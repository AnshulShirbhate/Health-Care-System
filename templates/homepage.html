Working of our Application


Our Healthcare management system or the Fitpros application is a user interactive web-application that that works like a health advisory system. For example, when we have a disease or a health issue we go to the doctor/advisor in a normal scenario, after that by listening to our problems and symtomps, they provide us the cure or treatement to recover from the issue that we are facing.

Our Web-application work the same but in an efficient and user-friendly manner. It takes user's disease/health issue or their physical and medical details as input through the registration form disease registration. After that it will automatically calculate and generate the infomation and exercise plan which includes the basic disease information, causes, symptoms, prevention tips and lastly but not leastly the recommended exercises calculated by the Application itself for the user.
